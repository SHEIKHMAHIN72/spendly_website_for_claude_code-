Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, such as your name and email address. We also store the expense data you enter into the App, including amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers or credit card details.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and helping you understand your spending patterns. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. You can delete your account and associated data at any time.

Third Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third parties. The App does not integrate with external analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Any future integrations will be communicated clearly, and you will have the option to opt out.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to our support team at support@spendly.app.
